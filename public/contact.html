About ShrinkMyPhoto

ShrinkMyPhoto is a free online image compression tool designed to help you reduce image file sizes without compromising quality. Our tool processes images entirely in your browser, ensuring your privacy and security.

Key Features:
  • 100% free to use
  • No registration required
  • Secure - images never leave your device
  • Support for JPG, PNG, and WebP formats
  • Batch processing capabilities
  • Multiple compression quality options

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is important to us. All image processing happens locally in your browser - we never upload, store, or access your images. Your files remain completely private and secure.
